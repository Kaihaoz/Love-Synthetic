# Love, Synthetic: AI Progressive Emotional Generation & Visualization Product
## 📖 Project Overview
This is a **complete design solution for an AI emotional interaction product**, addressing the core user pain point of "lack of layered, progressive expression" in daily emotional communication.

Unlike traditional AI writing tools that generate "one-time, uncontextualized content", I proposed a product form of "30-day progressive emotional love letter generation + emotional trajectory visualization" through a complete product loop of "needs insight → strategy design → solution verification", verifying the productization potential of AI in personalized emotional expression and cultural & creative interaction scenarios.

## 🎯 Core Pain Points & Product Positioning
### ❌ Core User Pain Points (Summarized from User Research)
1. **Expressive Gap**: Ordinary users struggle to output "layered, progressive" content in emotional expression (e.g., love letters, anniversary blessings), mostly producing fragmented expressions;
2. **Algorithm Black Box**: Existing AI emotional generation tools only output results, and users cannot perceive the "change logic of emotional intensity/style", reducing trust in usage;
3. **Poor Scene Adaptation**: General AI-generated content lacks "time dimension" design and cannot match the real scene demand of "emotions deepening over time".

### ✅ Product Positioning
Targeting young users (18-30 years old) with emotional expression needs, providing "AI-assisted progressive emotional content generation + visualization" services, focusing on "warm, contextual emotional expression", differentiated from general AI writing tools.

## 🔨 Core Product Design (Full Process)
As an independent product designer, completed the full-link work from "user research to solution implementation planning":

### 1. Needs Mining & Analysis
- **User Interviews**: Interviewed 15 target users and summarized the core demand of "lack of layered emotional expression, hoping AI-generated content is more in line with real emotional changes";
- **Competitive Analysis**: Analyzed 8 mainstream AI writing tools and found that "one-time generation, no time dimension, no visualization" are common industry problems;
- **Requirement Prioritization**: Through the MoSCoW method, identified "progressive generation" as the core requirement and "emotional visualization" as a high-priority experience requirement.

### 2. Core Strategy Design
#### (1) Emotional Progression Rule Design
Defined a "6-dimensional emotional progression model" (Shyness → Heartbeat → Sweetness → Longing → Deep Affection → Commitment) and planned the 30-day emotional evolution rhythm:
| Stage       | Time Range | Emotional Style       | Generation Constraints                |
|------------|------------|-----------------------|---------------------------------------|
| Germination | Day 1-7    | Implicit, tentative   | Forbid straightforward emotional words, short length |
| Warming Up  | Day 8-15   | Sweet, proactive      | Increase interactive expressions, medium length |
| Deepening   | Day 16-30  | Deep affection, committed | Combine memories/future, long length |

#### (2) Prompt Engineering Design
Designed a dedicated Prompt strategy pool for different stages to ensure generated content aligns with emotional progression rules:
- Germination Stage Example: "Write a subtle daily greeting in a friend's tone with a slight sense of heartbeat, no more than 50 words";
- Deepening Stage Example: "Write a deep confession combined with 30 days of memories, reflecting the commitment of long-term companionship, 100-150 words".

#### (3) Visualization Solution Design
Designed an "emotional trajectory visualization" solution to transform abstract emotions into intuitive visual presentation:
- Core Dimensions: Emotional intensity (0-10 points), emotional style (6 dimensions);
- Visual Form: Line chart (30-day emotional intensity change) + scatter plot (semantic clustering);
- Design Goal: Let users "see" the changing context of emotions and enhance trust in AI-generated content.

### 3. Product Implementation Planning
#### (1) Technical Path Planning
Clarified the core technology selection and phase division for product implementation:
- Basic Layer: Fine-tune GPT-2 with Chinese emotional corpus to adapt to emotional generation scenarios;
- Algorithm Layer: Extract text emotional vectors with Sentence-BERT and implement visualization via t-SNE dimensionality reduction;
- Product Layer: Implement in 2 phases (V1: Progressive generation; V2: Emotional visualization).

#### (2) Experience Design Principles
- Privacy: User-generated content is stored locally only, no cloud transmission;
- Controllability: Users can adjust the speed of emotional progression and customize emotional styles at key nodes;
- Understandability: The visualization interface is equipped with "emotional interpretation" copy to reduce user understanding costs.

## 📊 Key Deliverables
1. Needs analysis report (including user interview minutes, competitive analysis, requirement list);
2. Emotional progression rule manual (6-dimensional model + 30-day rhythm table);
3. Prompt strategy pool (30 groups of core Prompts designed by stage);
4. Visualization solution design draft (wireframe + interaction logic);
5. Technical implementation path planning document.

3. **Implementation Reference**: The complete design solution can be directly reused in vertical fields such as cultural creation, emotional companionship, and love education.
